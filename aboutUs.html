About Us Page

At Emtec, community involvement is a key part of our culture..

We take seriously our responsibility to the communities in which we live and work, and our Associates regularly give their time and resources to support local and national charities. Aside from being the lifeblood of Emtec, our Associates are also at the forefront of the civic, charitable, and philanthropic organizations in which they serve. Emtec provides its Associates with many opportunities to become involved with current efforts and also regularly initiates new projects to give back to the community..
